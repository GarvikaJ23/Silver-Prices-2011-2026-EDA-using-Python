II. Statistical Storytelling: Why are silver prices increasing?

Now let us look at what the silver market data tells us.
1. A Steady Baseline Erupting into a Massive Bull Run :The silver market has usually been pretty calm.
The average closing price for silver is around 23.50. The median price is a bit lower at 21.74.
When we look at the highest and lowest prices we see that something big happened recently.
The lowest price for silver was 11.735.. Then the price went up very fast near the end of the time period we are looking at.
By mid-January 2026 the highest price for silver was 93.00. The closing price was 91.87.
This means that if someone bought silver at its old price they would have seen the value go up by almost 800% by 2026. The silver market data shows us this.
